CS3113 - Final Project
=========

Final Project - Glow Hockey

  * Simple Glow Hockey

  * Movement in all directions, but cannot move past middle

  * Velocity of pad will transfer to velocity of puck

  * Particle and glow effects upon collision

  * AI

  * Different size maps

  * Controller support
